About

Hi, I'm Gina!

Poop scientist by trade; engineer by design. I'm driven by unmet public health need, inspired by mindfulness, and attracted to simplicity.

I tell product stories by researching what people need, brainstorming ideas, prototyping, and iterating based on user feedback. My process is flexible so that my design account for the ecosystem while addressing distinct needs.

When I am not reimagining systems, you can generally find me doodling, touting the superiority of homemade tomato sauce over Ragu, or somewhere on a mountain--skiing, biking, running, or climbing.
