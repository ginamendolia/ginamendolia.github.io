Design Philosophy

Nimble Thoughtfulness

always being mindfully observant

In today’s world of design, nimble thoughtfulness means purposefully thinking about the situations in which people, technology, and business overlap. I look for the places where the design needs to connect to be effective.

Effective connections are intuitive to the user while empathetic to the rest of the system. 

While making these connections, I belive it’s imperative to keep the operations of technology, the context in people live, and the demand that drives business in mind.
